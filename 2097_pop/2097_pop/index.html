Project Overview

Billionaire Statistics Analysis

The primary objective of this project is to analyze a dataset of billionaires to uncover insights regarding their demographics, wealth distribution, sources of wealth, and geographic distribution.

The analysis aims to identify patterns and trends among the world's wealthiest individuals, offering a deeper understanding of the factors that contribute to their wealth and how they are distributed globally.

Introducing CSEL 302

Introducing CSEL 302: Introduction to Intelligent Systems

CSEL 302 offers students a solid grasp of intelligent systems, crucial for contemporary technological progress. In this course, students delve into the fundamental principles, algorithms, and practical uses of intelligent systems, encompassing artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics. Through exploring vital concepts and techniques, students acquire an understanding of the design, implementation, and utilization of intelligent systems across diverse domains. This course provides learners with the necessary expertise to adapt to the changing realm of intelligent technologies and primes them for deeper exploration in this dynamic field.
